Special Guests

Chris Green

Christopher is a certified personal trainer and nutrition coach. He'll be offering a free mobility clinic at the jam to help athletes move better, recover faster, and prevent injuries. He has over 10 years of experience in resistance training and 14 years in tumbling, including 3 years as a parkour athlete. After recovering from a major injury, he's gained valuable insight into mobility, recovery, and rebuilding from the ground up. If you've got questions or are looking for new warmup exercises before you get started, look for him at the jam and he'll help you out!
