What is Columbus Parkour Jam?

Columbus Jam is an annual parkour and tricking gathering that started in 2023. Likeminded parkour and tricking athletes gather in central Ohio to train alongside some of the best pro athletes across the country. This year's jam will be Starting on Friday, August 15-17 2025. The event is hosted by Unorthodox Parkour and Sponsored by Altitude Martial Arts.
